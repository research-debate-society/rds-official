Research & Publishing
Abdul Rehman
Islamia College, Peshawar — Youth Activist from District Kurram

Abdul Rehman is an undergraduate student at Islamia College, Peshawar and a youth activist from District Kurram. His research focuses on the politics and governance of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and the former FATA region, with particular attention to the consequences of the 25th Constitutional Amendment. He is a co-author of the peer-reviewed article on proxy wars in Kurram published in Regional Studies (Winter 2025).
